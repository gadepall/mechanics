The given line parameters are
\begin{align}
		\vec{n} = \myvec{1\\-7},\, c=-5 
\end{align}
yielding
\begin{align}
	\vec{m} =\myvec{7 \\ 1}
\end{align}
The perpendicular passes through
\begin{align}
	\vec{A} &=\myvec{3\\0}
\end{align}
Hence, the desired equation is
		\begin{align}
			\myvec{7 & 1}\brak{\vec{x}-\myvec{3\\0}} &=0\\
		\implies 	\myvec{7 & 1}\vec{x} &= 21
		\end{align}
