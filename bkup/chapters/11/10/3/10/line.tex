\begin{align}
\vec{A}=\myvec{h\\ 3},\vec{B}=\myvec{4\\ 1} 
\implies \vec{B}-\vec{A}=
\myvec{4-h\\ -2}
\end{align}
The given line equation  can be expressed as
\begin{align}
\myvec{7& -9}\vec{x}&=19
\end{align}
yielding 
\begin{align}
\vec{n}=\myvec{7\\ -9},
\vec{m}=\myvec{9\\ 7}
\end{align}
Thus, 
\begin{align}
	\vec{m}^\top\brak{\vec{B}- \vec{A}}&=0\\
\implies\myvec{9& 7}\myvec{4-h\\ -2}&=0\\
\implies h&=\frac{22}{9}
\end{align}
